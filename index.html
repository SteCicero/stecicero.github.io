About me

My name is Stefano Cicero, I was born in Oristano, Italy, in 1987. Since I had my first PC (in 1999), my main interest has always been information technology. After high school I began studying Computer Engineering at University of Pisa, this provided me a deep all-around knowledge about ICT thematics. After having obtained the Bachelor’s Degree I started the Master’s Degree course in Computer Engineering. I also worked for 3 years as software engineer and sysadmin for the .it Registry in the National Research Council (CNR) in Pisa. Currently I’m focused on finishing the Master’s Degree course.
